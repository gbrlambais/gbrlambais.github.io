Guilherme Lambais

I'm an Assistent Professor of Economics at Lusíada University of Lisbon, Faculty of Economics and Management, where I'm the coordinator of the undergraduate economics course. At Lusíada, I co-founded and am currently the co-director of the Center for Research in Economics and Comparative Development, leading the reconstruction of the Brazilian historical GDP. I'm also the organizer of the Lisbon Economic History and Development Seminars. I'm interested in economic development & growth, economic history, and political economy.
