Guilherme Lambais

I'm co-organizing "The New Economic History of Brazil" conference in September 2025. See: call for papers and program (in June).

I'm an Assistent Professor of Economics at Lusíada University of Lisbon, Faculty of Economics and Management, where I'm the coordinator of the undergraduate economics course.

At Lusíada, I co-founded and am currently the deputy director of the Center for Research in Economics and Comparative Development, leading the reconstruction of the Brazilian historical GDP. I'm also the organizer of the Lisbon Economic History and Development Seminars.

I'm interested in economic development & growth, economic history, and political economy. More about me and my research. My CV.
